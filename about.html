About Me

I am a London-based music producer and songwriter, passionate about many kinds of music from modern rock to 19th century classical and almost everything in between. I believe that the best kinds of music come from unfiltered self-expression, so I aim to highlight the individuality of the artists I work with most of all. I also have a deep love for video game soundtracks and the unique sound design within the medium.

Skills

  • Experienced with Logic Pro and Ableton Live
  • Hands-on experience with the SSL Duality SE mixing console
  • Proficient in sound design and mixing techniques
  • Strong understanding of music theory and composition
  • Collaborative and adaptable in creative environments
